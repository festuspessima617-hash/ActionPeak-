About ActionPeak

Driving awareness, protection, and support across Sierra Leone.

We combine community education, survivor advocacy, and trusted reporting channels to end gender-based violence.

Who we are

ActionPeak is a community-centered organization dedicated to protecting women, girls, and children through education, prevention, and immediate support. Our programs are designed to be accessible, trustworthy, and effective across different regions.

Our Vision

To build a safer society where every person is respected, heard, and free from violence.

Our Mission

Educating communities, empowering survivors, and supporting local networks to prevent abuse and respond with care.

Our Values

Respect, dignity, accountability, and access to justice for everyone.
